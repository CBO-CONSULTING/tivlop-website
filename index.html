The story
"I just wanted a simple app for my Carrefour card."

The app I used for my loyalty cards got acquired and turned into something I didn't sign up for. Shopping features, payment services, stuff I never asked for. I just wanted to scan my card at checkout. So I built Tivlop. A digital wallet that does one job well, without trying to sell you anything. Made solo in Brussels.
